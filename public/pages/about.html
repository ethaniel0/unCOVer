unCOVer

unCOVer was developed during the Space Apps competition. It aims to "uncover" any correlations between air quality, temperature, etc. and COVID-19.

Dependencies

Many different libraries and modules made this possible, including, but not limited to:

  • Express.js
  • Chart.js
  • Arcgis.js
  • simply_clean.css
  • Font Awesome
  • APIs/Datasets for relevant information

Data Sources

Datasets were processed and synthesized from detailed records into JSON files for easier usage. For example, COVID-19 data was sourced from John Hopkins. Although we generally use the processed files to display data onto the map, we also used real time APIs, such as converting locations into longitude/latitude.
